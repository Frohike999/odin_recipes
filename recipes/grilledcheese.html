Grilled Cheese

Ingredients

  • 2 slices sourdough bread
  • Mayonnaise
  • 1 to 2 ounces sharp cheddar cheese
  • 1 tablespoon of butter for pan

Steps

  1. Heat pan to medium-low
  2. Spread mayo on one side of bread
  3. Put bread mayo side down on skillet. Top with cheese
  4. After 3-4 minutes flip one slice of bread onto the other and remove from skillet.
