Mashed Potatoes

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs potatoes peeled
  • Milk
  • 8 tablespoons of butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. In a large pot of water boil potatoes until soft, about 20 minutes.
  2. Drain pot into a collander. After draining, put potatoes in large bowl
  3. Mash the potatoes
  4. Add a splash of milk, salt and pepper, and 4 tablespoons of butter. Stir until butter is melted into potatoes.
  5. Taste and add salt and pepper as needed. Continue adding butter to potatoes to melt in.
